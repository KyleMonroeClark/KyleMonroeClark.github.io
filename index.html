About Me

Hi, I’m Kyle — I’ve always been curious about how things work, but more importantly, why they work.

That curiosity has led me to a career at the intersection of data, systems, and people. With a background in quantitative analysis, I’ve helped optimize operations across healthcare and hospitality — using data to uncover patterns, shape decisions, and ultimately, tell stories that drive real change. I believe the best insights come not just from technical accuracy, but from understanding the full context around a problem and communicating findings in a way that others can act on.

What sets me apart is my ability to move fluently between technical detail and human insight. I’ve built systems to streamline patient care logistics, developed reporting tools for executive decision-making, and supported professionals working through complex certification programs. In every project, I strive to connect the numbers to the bigger picture — to find the signal in the noise, and to share it in a way that makes sense to real people.

I’m looking for a team that values both precision and perspective — one where I can apply my diverse technical and communication skills to make a meaningful impact.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio — I hope it gives you a sense of how I work, how I think, and what I care about.
